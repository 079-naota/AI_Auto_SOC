We stayed here for one night after getting married. The location is wonderful (on a beautiful beach, with a park nearby and restaurants). We took an evening walk, it was so beautiful and romantic. 
We spent the evening at the restaurant on site. The food was really tasty (we chose the tasting menu). It was a quiet and peaceful evening, we enjoyed it.
The staff was really polite and friendly.
The room was clean and nice, but the size was a bit disappointing. However, it was fine for one night.
The breakfast was okay, but not fantastic.

The only real downside was the lack of parking. We were told to park in the street, which wasn't convenient. It made the start of our honeymoon a bit stressful.

Overall, it's a great hotel for a romantic night or two, especially if you want to enjoy the beach and the local area. But be aware of the parking situation.
